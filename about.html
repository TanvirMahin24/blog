About Natai

Natai, is a Bangladeshi youth organization with the sole purpose to provide students a common platform for showcasing their creative skills.

Team Natai inviting students to be the part of a journey to the brightest sight of our life full of happiness, satisfaction and love.

Our goal and Services

In this platform, students can build up communities with their fellows. Through Natai, they will have their own secured profile and the proprietary rights of their creations.

An online magazine will be published monthly selecting the best articles of the students along with the works from famous writers, speakers and enthusiastic personnel of our country.
